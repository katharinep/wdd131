Genealogy Blog Site Plan

Katharine Yuan

WDD 131-A2

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to be a genealogy blog as a form of self promotion for research services. It will include articles and a page with mock links to documents to help with research, including "cheat sheets" and research forms (research logs, evidence analysis chart, etc.)

Audience

My main audience is potential clients. My secondary audience is other professionals for the purpose of building credibility and opening career opportunities.

Dynamic elements

There will be a form for service inquiries (conditional branching). There will be a drop down menu to select reason for inquiry. (record lookup, research project, speaking opportunities) There will also be event listeners on the child site that watches for clicking on document links. The article data will be saved in a separate .js file. A simple search bar ought to fulfill the object and array requirements.
